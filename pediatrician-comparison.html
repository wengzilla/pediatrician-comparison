Top Recommendations

1Dr. Lori Kandel — Sunnyvale
4.9–5.0 stars across all platforms with 20+ reviews. 26 years of experience. Trained at Children's Hospital LA. Consistently described as thoughtful, kind, responsive, and amazing with children. Accepting new patients. The strongest combination of ratings, experience, and availability.
2Dr. Douglas Kaye — Sunnyvale
4.6–5.0 stars with 170+ survey responses on Sutter Health alone. 37 years of experience (Wake Forest / U. of Chicago). Praised for exceptional listening, thorough explanations, and long-term patient loyalty. A very safe, experienced choice.
3Dr. Marisa Cappiello — Sunnyvale
5 stars on Vitals, ~4 on Yelp with 17–20 reviews. 20+ years experience (Georgetown / Stanford). Known for exceptional follow-up care, weekend calls, and running the Pediatric Growing Healthy Clinic. Great if nutrition/wellness is a priority.
4Dr. Yi Ding — Mountain View
5.0 stars on every platform reviewed. Trained at UW–Madison and UCSF. FAAP-designated. Praised for thoroughness, follow-up calls, and empathy with new parents. Fewer reviews but uniformly excellent. May not be accepting new patients — worth calling.
5Dr. Lucy Schoen — Mountain View
5.0 on Yelp (11 reviews, nearly all 5-star). Stanford-trained. Patients love her warmth with kids and communication style. Also may not be accepting new patients — confirm with office.
